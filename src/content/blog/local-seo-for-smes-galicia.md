## Why is local SEO crucial for your business?

If you run a business in A Coruña, Santiago, Ferrol or anywhere in Galicia, potential customers are searching for you on Google right now. Searches like "mechanic A Coruña", "restaurant near me" or "tax advisor Santiago" generate thousands of visits per month.

The problem is that if your business doesn't show up in those results, those customers go to your competitors.

## What is local SEO?

Local SEO is the set of strategies that make your business appear in Google's geolocated searches. This includes:

- **Google Business Profile**: your business listing on Google Maps
- **Website optimization**: ensuring your page correctly mentions your location and services
- **Reviews**: customer opinions directly impact your ranking
- **Local citations**: appearing in directories like Yellow Pages, Yelp, etc.

## 5 steps to improve your local SEO

### 1. Claim and optimize your Google Business Profile

It's free and it's the first thing you should do. Make sure to:
- Complete ALL fields (hours, address, phone, website)
- Upload real, quality photos of your business
- Choose the correct categories
- Post weekly updates

### 2. Optimize your website for local searches

Your website should include:
- Your city and region name in titles and copy
- A contact page with full address and map
- LocalBusiness schema markup
- Meta descriptions mentioning your location

### 3. Get reviews from real customers

Google reviews are one of the most important ranking factors:
- Ask satisfied customers for reviews (a direct link makes it easy)
- Respond to ALL reviews, positive and negative
- Never buy fake reviews (Google penalizes this)

### 4. Create relevant local content

A blog with content related to your area helps you rank:
- Write about local events related to your industry
- Create useful guides for your community
- Mention neighborhoods, regions and local references

### 5. Maintain NAP consistency

NAP stands for Name, Address, Phone. This information must be EXACTLY the same across:
- Your website
- Google Business Profile
- Social media
- Local directories

## How long does it take to work?

Local SEO isn't instant, but results are usually seen faster than traditional SEO. In our experience with clients in Galicia:

- **1-2 months**: improvements in Google Business Profile and Maps
- **3-4 months**: improvements in local organic results
- **6+ months**: solid, stable positioning
